Event Overview

Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology proudly hosts an electrifying startup fest, a convergence of innovation and entrepreneurship. This event promises a dynamic platform for budding entrepreneurs, seasoned professionals, and investors alike. Through a myriad of meticulously planned activities, including round table discussions, mentorship sessions, and tech talks, participants delve into the depths of entrepreneurial wisdom. From idea pitching competitions to product exhibitions, the fest celebrates creativity and ingenuity. With MoU signings, investor connects, and intern networking opportunities, it fosters invaluable collaborations. Led by Sathyabama's commitment to nurturing talent, this event is a beacon illuminating the path towards a vibrant startup ecosystem.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Sathyabama Startup-Fest 2024 a career fair?
Leave your resumes at home because the Tech Expo is not a career fair. The Tech Expo will host engineering staffs and manufacturing department employees. The Tech Expo is an opportunity for companies to promote their businesses, display what they’re working on and network with manufacturers, suppliers, clients and customers. It’s everyone’s opportunity to “talk shop.”
Who should attend the Sathyabama Startup Fest 2024?
Manufacturers, suppliers, clients and customers who want to connect with other manufacturers, suppliers, clients and customers should attend Tech Expo. Companies that are interested in seeing the capabilities of the students will also benefit from attending.
Where is the events and what are the timings?
The event will take place at Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology, Chennai, Tamil Nadu. For event details please feel free to explore the event section
Do we have to fill both the registration forms?
If you are a participant of any event fill the form with respective of the event. If you are a visitor then fill the visitors form. Filling both form is not nessasary
My business is small — just a few people. Should I attend the Event
Yes! Here’s your chance to network with people from businesses of all sizes.
Is it free?
Yes, We focus on providing platform for all people to disclose their talents, Hence the tickets are free!
